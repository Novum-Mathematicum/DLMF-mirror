In terms of the normal form ( 36.2.1 ) the $\Psi_{K}\left(\mathbf{x}\right)$ satisfy the operator equation


<a id="E1"></a>
$$
\Phi_{K}'\left(-i\frac{\partial}{\partial x_{1}};\mathbf{x}\right)\Psi_{K}\left(\mathbf{x}\right)=0, \tag{36.10.1}

<a id="E2"></a>

$$
