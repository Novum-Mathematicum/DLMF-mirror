$$

$$

where

<a id="E2"></a>

\displaystyle x_{k} \displaystyle=\tfrac{1}{4}(\nu-\mu-k+1)(\nu+\mu+k)\left(x^{2}-1\right), \tag{14.14.2}
$$

<a id="Ex2"></a>
$$
\displaystyle y_{k} \displaystyle=(\mu+k)x,
$$
