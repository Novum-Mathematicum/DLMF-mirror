§34.5(ii) Symmetry

ⓘ
Keywords:
6⁢j symbols, Regge symmetries, 6⁢j symbols, symmetry
Notes:

The 6⁢j symbol is invariant under interchange of any two columns and also under interchange of the upper and lower arguments in each of any two columns, for example,

34.5.8 {j1j2j3l1l2l3}={j2j1j3l2l1l3}={j1l2l3l1j2j3}.
