Error bounds for (11.11.8) and (11.11.10) are given in Meijer (1932) and Nemes (2014b, c). The later references also contain exponentially-improved extensions of (11.11.8) and (11.11.10). For an extension of (11.11.17) (and (11.11.16)) into a complete asymptotic expansion, see Nemes (2020).

When ν is real and positive, all of (11.11.10)–(11.11.17) can be regarded as special cases of two asymptotic expansions given in Olver (1997b, pp. 352–360) for 𝐀−ν⁡(λ⁢ν) as ν→+∞, one being uniform for 0<λ≤1, and the other being uniform for λ≥1. (Note that Olver’s definition of 𝐀ν⁡(z) omits the factor 1/π in (11.10.4).) See also Watson (1944, §10.15).
