§21.6(i) Riemann Identity

ⓘ
Keywords:
Riemann identity, Riemann theta functions, Riemann theta functions with characteristics, equivalent, matrix, vector
Notes:

Let 𝐓=[Tj⁢k] be an arbitrary h×h orthogonal matrix (that is, 𝐓⁢𝐓T=𝐈) with rational elements. Also, let 𝐙 be an arbitrary g×h matrix. Define

21.6.1 𝒦=ℤg×h⁢𝐓/(ℤg×h⁢𝐓∩ℤg×h),

21.6.2 𝒟=|𝐓T⁢ℤh/(𝐓T⁢ℤh∩ℤh)|,
