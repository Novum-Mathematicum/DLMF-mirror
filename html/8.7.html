8.7.6 Γ⁡(a,x)=xa⁢e−x⁢∑n=0∞Ln(a)⁡(x)n+1,
x>0, ℜ⁡a<12.
ⓘ
Symbols:
Ln(α)⁡(x): Laguerre (or generalized Laguerre) polynomial, e: base of natural logarithm, Γ⁡(a,z): incomplete gamma function, ℜ⁡: real part, x: real variable, z: complex variable, a: parameter and n: nonnegative integer
Proof sketch:
Integrate (18.12.13) from z=0 to z=1 and for the integral on the left-hand side use the substitution z=t1+t. The resulting integral is (8.6.5). The constraint ℜ⁡a<12 follows from (18.15.14).
