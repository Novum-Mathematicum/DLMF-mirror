9.5.3 Bi⁡(x)=1π⁢∫0∞exp⁡(−13⁢t3+x⁢t)⁢dt+1π⁢∫0∞sin⁡(13⁢t3+x⁢t)⁢dt.
ⓘ
Symbols:
Bi⁡(z): Airy function, π: the ratio of the circumference of a circle to its diameter, [a,b): half-closed interval, dx: differential of x, exp⁡z: exponential function, e: base of natural logarithm, i: imaginary unit, ∫: integral, (a,b]: half-closed interval, sin⁡z: sine function and x: real variable
Proof sketch:
Use (9.5.5) with the substitions: for the paths (−∞,0] use t=−τ, for the paths [0,∞⁢e±π⁢i/3) use t=e±π⁢i/3⁢τ. For the resulting integrals use (4.14.1) and (4.14.2).
