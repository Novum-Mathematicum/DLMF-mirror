§26.5(i) Definitions

ⓘ
Keywords:
Catalan numbers, definitions, relation to lattice paths
Notes:
See Comtet (1974, pp. 52–54). Table 26.5.1 was computed by the author.

Annotations for §26.5 and Ch.26

C⁡(n) is the Catalan number. It counts the number of lattice paths from (0,0) to (n,n) that stay on or above the line y=x.

26.5.1 C⁡(n)=1n+1⁢(2⁢nn)=12⁢n+1⁢(2⁢n+1n)=(2⁢nn)−(2⁢nn−1)=(2⁢n−1n)−(2⁢n−1n+1).
ⓘ
