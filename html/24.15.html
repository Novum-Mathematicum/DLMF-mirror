§24.15(iv) Fibonacci and Lucas Numbers

ⓘ
Keywords:
Fibonacci numbers, Lucas numbers
Notes:
See Kelisky (1957, pp. 32 and 34).
Permalink:
http://dlmf.nist.gov/24.15.iv
See also:
Annotations for §24.15 and Ch.24

The Fibonacci numbers are defined by u0=0, u1=1, and un+1=un+un−1, n≥1. The Lucas numbers are defined by v0=2, v1=1, and vn+1=vn+vn−1, n≥1.
