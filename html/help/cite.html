How to Cite

♦ Permalinks & Reference numbers ♦

When citing DLMF from a formal publication, we suggest a format similar to the following:

  1. [DLMF]

    NIST Digital Library of Mathematical Functions. https://dlmf.nist.gov/, Release 1.2.4 of 2025-03-15. F. W. J. Olver, A. B. Olde Daalhuis, D. W. Lozier, B. I. Schneider, R. F. Boisvert, C. W. Clark, B. R. Miller, B. V. Saunders, H. S. Cohl, and M. A. McClain, eds.

If you mention a specific equation (or chapter, section, …), you’ll help your readers find it by using our Permalinks & Reference numbers. For example, the sentence in the LaTeX source:

For the gamma function at positive integers see \cite[\href{https://dlmf.nist.gov/5.4.E1}{(5.4.1)}]{NIST:DLMF}; for its extrema see \cite[\href{https://dlmf.nist.gov/5.4.T1}{Table 5.4.1}]{NIST:DLMF}.

produces pdf with the equation and table numbers linking directly into the DLMF website:

For the gamma function at positive integers, see [DLMF, (5.4.1)]; for its extrema see [DLMF, Table 5.4.1].

(assuming \usepackage{hyperref} in the preamble, and NIST:DLMF being the key for DLMF)

This BibTeX item may be useful:

⬇
@misc{NIST:DLMF,
key = "{\relax DLMF}",
title = "{\it NIST Digital Library of Mathematical Functions}",
howpublished = "\url{https://dlmf.nist.gov/}, Release 1.2.4 of 2025-03-15",
url = "https://dlmf.nist.gov/",
note = "F.~W.~J. Olver, A.~B. {Olde Daalhuis}, D.~W. Lozier, B.~I. Schneider,
R.~F. Boisvert, C.~W. Clark, B.~R. Miller, B.~V. Saunders,
H.~S. Cohl, and M.~A. McClain, eds."}

Citations from other electronic media (the web, email, …), should, of course, use the appropriate means to give the site URL (https://dlmf.nist.gov/), or specific Permalinks.

Permalinks & Reference numbers

The direct correspondence between the reference numbers in the printed Handbook and the permalinks used online in the DLMF enables readers of either version to cite specific items and their readers to easily look them up again — in either version!

The following table outlines the correspondence between reference numbers as they appear in the Handbook, and the URL’s that find the same item online. Note the ‘E’, ‘F’ and ‘T’ used to disambiguate equations, figures and tables.

Item Type Ref. Number Permalink
Chapters 5 https://dlmf.nist.gov/5
Sections 5.4 https://dlmf.nist.gov/5.4
Subsections 5.4(iii) https://dlmf.nist.gov/5.4.iii
Equations 5.4.1 https://dlmf.nist.gov/5.4.E1
Figures 5.3.1 https://dlmf.nist.gov/5.3.F1
Tables 5.4.1 https://dlmf.nist.gov/5.4.T1

For convenience, the permalink can be found in the pop-up ‘Info box’ associated with each item in the site.
