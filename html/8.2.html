§8.2(ii) Analytic Continuation

In this subsection the functions γ and Γ have their general values.

The function γ∗⁡(a,z) is entire in z and a. When z≠0, Γ⁡(a,z) is an entire function of a, and γ⁡(a,z) is meromorphic with simple poles at a=−n, n=0,1,2,…, with residue (−1)n/n!.

For m∈ℤ,

8.2.8 γ⁡(a,z⁢e2⁢π⁢m⁢i) =e2⁢π⁢m⁢i⁢a⁢γ⁡(a,z),
