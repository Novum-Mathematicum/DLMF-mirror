Other reductions of H⁢ℓ to a F12, with at least one free parameter, exist iff the pair (a,p) takes one of a finite number of values, where q=α⁢β⁢p. Below are three such reductions with three and two parameters. They are analogous to quadratic and cubic hypergeometric transformations (§§15.8(iii)–15.8(v)).
