Let fn⁡(z) denote any of 𝗃n⁡(z), 𝗒n⁡(z), 𝗁n(1)⁡(z), or 𝗁n(2)⁡(z). Then

10.51.1 fn−1⁡(z)+fn+1⁡(z) =((2⁢n+1)/z)⁢fn⁡(z),
n⁢fn−1⁡(z)−(n+1)⁢fn+1⁡(z) =(2⁢n+1)⁢fn′⁡(z),
n=1,2,…,
ⓘ
Symbols:
n: integer, z: complex variable and fn⁡(z): a spherical Bessel function
