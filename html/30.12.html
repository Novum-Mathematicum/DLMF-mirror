§30.12 Generalized and Coulomb Spheroidal Functions

ⓘ
Keywords:
Coulomb, Coulomb spheroidal functions, generalized, spheroidal wave functions
Referenced by:
§31.12
Permalink:

Generalized spheroidal wave functions and Coulomb spheroidal functions are solutions of the differential equation

30.12.1 ddz⁡((1−z2)⁢dwdz)+(λ+α⁢z+γ2⁢(1−z2)−μ21−z2)⁢w=0,
ⓘ
Symbols:
dfdx: derivative of f with respect to x, z: complex variable, w: solution to DE, λ: real parameter, γ2: real parameter and μ: real parameter
Referenced by:
§30.12
Permalink:
http://dlmf.nist.gov/30.12.E1
Encodings:

Annotations for §30.12 and Ch.30

which reduces to (30.2.1) if α=0. Equation (30.12.1) appears in astrophysics and molecular physics. For the theory and computation of solutions of (30.12.1) see Falloon (2001), Judd (1975), Leaver (1986), and Komarov et al. (1976).

Another generalization is provided by the differential equation

30.12.2 ddz⁡((1−z2)⁢dwdz)+(λ+γ2⁢(1−z2)−α⁢(α+1)z2−μ21−z2)⁢w=0,
ⓘ
Symbols:
dfdx: derivative of f with respect to x, z: complex variable, w: solution to DE, λ: real parameter, γ2: real parameter and μ: real parameter

which also reduces to (30.2.1) when α=0. See Leitner and Meixner (1960), Slepian (1964) with μ=0, and Meixner et al. (1980).
