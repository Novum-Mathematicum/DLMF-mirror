§14.13 Trigonometric Expansions

ⓘ
Keywords:
Ferrers functions, trigonometric expansions
Notes:
The expansions (14.13.1) and (14.13.2) are derived in §3.5 of Erdélyi et al. (1953a), but without any statement of conditions on ν and μ. To obtain these conditions we see that by combination of (14.13.1) and (14.13.2),
±12⁢π⁢i⁢𝖯νμ⁡(cos⁡θ)+𝖰νμ⁡(cos⁡θ)=π12⁢Γ⁡(ν+μ+1)⁢(2⁢sin⁡θ)μ⁢e±(ν+μ+1)⁢i⁢θ⁢𝐅⁡(ν+μ+1,μ+12;ν+32;e±2⁢i⁢θ).
We then apply the results given in the last paragraph of §15.2(i), and subsequently take real and imaginary parts in the case of conditional convergence.

Correction (effective with 1.1.3):
The conditions for convergence of the Fourier expansions (14.13.1), (14.13.2) have been corrected. Originally it was stated that they converge “…conditionally when ν is real and 0≤μ<12.” It has been corrected to read “If 0≤ℜ⁡μ<12 then they converge, but, if θ≠12⁢π, they do not converge absolutely.”

Suggested 2021-09-08 by Hans Volkmer

Clarification (effective with 1.0.1):
The conditions for convergence of the expansions in this subsection were clarified.
