Annotations for Ch.29
  • •

    Ince (1940a) tabulates the eigenvalues aνm⁡(k2), bνm+1⁡(k2) (with aν2⁢m+1 and bν2⁢m+1 interchanged) for k2=0.1,0.5,0.9, ν=−12,0⁢(1)⁢25, and m=0,1,2,3. Precision is 4D.

  • •

    Arscott and Khabaza (1962) tabulates the coefficients of the polynomials P in Table 29.12.1 (normalized so that the numerically largest coefficient is unity, i.e. monic polynomials), and the corresponding eigenvalues h for k2=0.1⁢(.1)⁢0.9, n=1⁢(1)⁢30. Equations from §29.6 can be used to transform to the normalization adopted in this chapter. Precision is 6S.
