§25.9 Asymptotic Approximations

ⓘ
Keywords:
Riemann zeta function, approximations, asymptotic
Notes:
See Titchmarsh (1986b, Sections 4.11–4.18).

If x≥1, y≥1, 2⁢π⁢x⁢y=t, and 0≤σ≤1, then as t→∞ with σ fixed,

25.9.1 ζ⁡(σ+i⁢t)=∑1≤n≤x1ns+χ⁢(s)⁢∑1≤n≤y1n1−s+O⁡(x−σ)+O⁡(yσ−1⁢t12−σ),
ⓘ
Symbols:
O⁡(x): order not exceeding, ζ⁡(s): Riemann zeta function, i: imaginary unit, n: nonnegative integer, x: real variable, s: complex variable and σ: fixed
