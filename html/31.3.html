H⁢ℓ⁡(a,q;α,β,γ,δ;z) denotes the solution of (31.2.1) that corresponds to the exponent 0 at z=0 and assumes the value 1 there. Solutions (31.3.1) and (31.3.5)–(31.3.11) comprise a set of 8 local solutions of (31.2.1): 2 per singular point. Each is related to the solution (31.3.1) by one of the automorphisms of §31.2(v).
