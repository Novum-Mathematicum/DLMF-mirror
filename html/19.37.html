ⓘ
See also:
Annotations for §19.37(iv), §19.37 and Ch.19

Tabulated for σ=0⁢(.05)⁢0.5⁢(.1)⁢1⁢(.2)⁢2⁢(.5)⁢5, cos⁡(3⁢γ)=−1⁢(.2)⁢1 to 5D by Carlson (1961a). Here σ2=23⁢((ln⁡a)2+(ln⁡b)2+(ln⁡c)2), cos⁡(3⁢γ)=(4/σ3)⁢(ln⁡a)⁢(ln⁡b)⁢(ln⁡c), and a,b,c are semiaxes of an ellipsoid with the same volume as the unit sphere.
