However, in these circumstances the orthogonality property (18.2.1) disappears. For this reason, and also in the interest of simplicity, in the case of the Jacobi polynomials Pn(α,β)⁡(x) we assume throughout this chapter that α>−1 and β>−1, unless stated otherwise.
