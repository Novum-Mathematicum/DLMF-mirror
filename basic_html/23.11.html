§23.11 Integral Representations

ⓘ

Weierstrass elliptic functions, integral representations
Notes:

23.11.1 f1⁡(s,τ) =cosh2⁡(12⁢τ⁢s)1−2⁢e−s⁢cosh⁡(τ⁢s)+e−2⁢s,
f2⁡(s,τ) =cos2⁡(12⁢s)1−2⁢ei⁢τ⁢s⁢cos⁡s+e2⁢i⁢τ⁢s.
