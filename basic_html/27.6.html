§27.6 Divisor Sums

ⓘ
Keywords:
divisor sums, number-theoretic functions
Notes:
See Apostol (1976, Chapter 2).
Permalink:
http://dlmf.nist.gov/27.6
See also:
Annotations for Ch.27

Sums of number-theoretic functions extended over divisors are of special interest. For example,

27.6.1 ∑d|nλ⁡(d)={1,n⁢ is a square,0,otherwise.
ⓘ
Symbols:
λ⁡(n): Liouville’s function, d: positive integer and n: positive integer
Permalink:
http://dlmf.nist.gov/27.6.E1

If f is multiplicative, then

27.6.2 ∑d|nμ⁡(d)⁢f⁡(d)=∏p|n(1−f⁡(p)),
n>1.
ⓘ
Symbols:
μ⁡(n): Möbius function, d: positive integer, n: positive integer and p,p1,…: prime numbers
