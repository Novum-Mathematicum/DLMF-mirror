Keywords:

Notes:
For (19.23.8) and (19.23.9) see Carlson (1977b, Exercises 5.9-19, 5.9-20, and p. 306). By §19.16(iii), (19.23.8) implies (19.23.1)–(19.23.3), and (19.23.9) implies (19.23.6).
