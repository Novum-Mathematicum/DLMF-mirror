For fixed z, other than ±1 or ∞, each branch of Pν−μ⁡(z) and 𝑸νμ⁡(z) is an entire function of each parameter ν and μ.

The behavior of Pν−μ⁡(z) and 𝑸νμ⁡(z) as z→−1 from the left on the upper or lower side of the cut from −∞ to 1 can be deduced from (14.8.7)–(14.8.11), combined with (14.24.1) and (14.24.2) with s=±1.
