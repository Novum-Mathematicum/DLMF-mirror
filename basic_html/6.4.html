Analytic continuation of the principal value of E1⁡(z) yields a multi-valued function with branch points at z=0 and z=∞. The general value of E1⁡(z) is given by

6.4.1 E1⁡(z)=Ein⁡(z)−Ln⁡z−γ;
ⓘ
Symbols:
γ: Euler’s constant, Ein⁡(z): complementary exponential integral, E1⁡(z): exponential integral, Ln⁡z: general logarithm function and z: complex variable
