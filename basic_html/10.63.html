§10.63(ii) Cross-Products

ⓘ
Keywords:
Kelvin functions, cross-products, recurrence relations
Notes:
Set a=x⁢e3⁢π⁢i/4. Then from (10.61.1) and (10.63.5) Jν⁡(a)⁢Jν⁡(a¯)=pν, Jν′⁡(a)⁢Jν′⁡(a¯)=sν, Jν⁡(a)⁢Jν′⁡(a¯)=e3⁢π⁢i/4⁢(rν−i⁢qν), Jν⁡(a¯)⁢Jν′⁡(a)=e−3⁢π⁢i/4⁢(rν+i⁢qν). Combine these results with (10.6.2) and eliminate the derivatives. See also Petiau (1955, pp. 266–267) (but this reference contains errors). For the functions kerν⁡x and keiν⁡x use the second of (10.61.2).
