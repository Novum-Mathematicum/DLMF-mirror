Numerical values of the radii of convergence ρn(j) of the power series (28.6.1)–(28.6.14) for n=0,1,…,9 are given in Table 28.6.1. Here j=1 for a2⁢n⁡(q), j=2 for b2⁢n+2⁡(q), and j=3 for a2⁢n+1⁡(q) and b2⁢n+1⁡(q). (Table 28.6.1 is reproduced from Meixner et al. (1980, §2.4).)

It is conjectured that for large n, the radii increase in proportion to the square of the eigenvalue number n; see Meixner et al. (1980, §2.4). It is known that

28.6.20 lim infn→∞ρn(j)n2≥k⁢k′⁢(K⁡(k))2=2.04183 4⁢…,
