For a uniform asymptotic expansion in terms of Airy functions for Wκ,μ⁡(4⁢κ⁢x) when κ is large and positive, μ is real with |μ| bounded, and x∈[δ,∞) see Olver (1997b, Chapter 11, Ex. 7.3). This expansion is simpler in form than the expansions of Dunster (1989) that correspond to the approximations given in §13.21(iii), but the conditions on μ are more restrictive.

For asymptotic expansions having double asymptotic properties see Skovgaard (1966).

See also §13.20(v).
