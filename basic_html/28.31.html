Asymptotic Behavior

ⓘ
Keywords:
asymptotic behavior for large variable, paraboloidal wave functions
See also:
Annotations for §28.31(iii), §28.31 and Ch.28

For ξ>0, the functions ℎ𝑐pm⁡(z,ξ), ℎ𝑠pm⁡(z,ξ) behave asymptotically as multiples of exp⁡(−14⁢ξ⁢cos⁡(2⁢z))⁢(cos⁡z)p as z→±i⁢∞. All other periodic solutions behave as multiples of exp⁡(14⁢ξ⁢cos⁡(2⁢z))⁢(cos⁡z)−p−2.

For ξ>0, the functions ℎ𝑐pm⁡(z,−ξ), ℎ𝑠pm⁡(z,−ξ) behave asymptotically as multiples of exp⁡(14⁢ξ⁢cos⁡(2⁢z))⁢(cos⁡z)−p−2 as z→12⁢π±i⁢∞. All other periodic solutions behave as multiples of exp⁡(−14⁢ξ⁢cos⁡(2⁢z))⁢(cos⁡z)p.
