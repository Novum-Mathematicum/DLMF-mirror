§32.4(i) Definition

ⓘ

PI–PVI can be expressed as the compatibility condition of a linear system, called an isomonodromy problem or Lax pair. Suppose

32.4.1 ∂𝚿∂λ =𝐀⁡(z,λ)⁢𝚿,
∂𝚿∂z =𝐁⁡(z,λ)⁢𝚿,

∂f∂x: partial derivative of f with respect to x, ∂x: partial differential of x and z: real

is a linear system in which 𝐀 and 𝐁 are matrices and λ is independent of z. Then the equation

32.4.2 ∂2𝚿∂z⁢∂λ=∂2𝚿∂λ⁢∂z,

∂x: partial differential of x and z: real

32.4.3 ∂𝐀∂z−∂𝐁∂λ+𝐀⁢𝐁−𝐁⁢𝐀=0.
