§33.13 Complex Variable and Parameters

ⓘ
Keywords:
Coulomb functions: variables ρ,η, complex variable and parameters
Referenced by:
§33.22(vii)
Permalink:
http://dlmf.nist.gov/33.13
See also:
Annotations for Ch.33

The functions Fℓ⁡(η,ρ), Gℓ⁡(η,ρ), and Hℓ±⁡(η,ρ) may be extended to noninteger values of ℓ by generalizing (2⁢ℓ+1)!=Γ⁡(2⁢ℓ+2), and supplementing (33.6.5) by a formula derived from (33.2.8) with U⁡(a,b,z) expanded via (13.2.42).

These functions may also be continued analytically to complex values of ρ, η, and ℓ. The quantities Cℓ⁡(η), σℓ⁡(η), and Rℓ, given by (33.2.6), (33.2.10), and (33.4.1), respectively, must be defined consistently so that

33.13.1 Cℓ⁡(η)=2ℓ⁢ei⁢σℓ⁡(η)−(π⁢η/2)⁢Γ⁡(ℓ+1−i⁢η)/Γ⁡(2⁢ℓ+2),
