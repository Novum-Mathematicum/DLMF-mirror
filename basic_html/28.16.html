§28.16 Asymptotic Expansions for Large q

ⓘ
Keywords:
Mathieu’s equation, asymptotic expansions for large q, eigenvalues (or characteristic values)
Notes:

Let s=2⁢m+1, m=0,1,2,…, and ν be fixed with m<ν<m+1. Then as h(=q)→+∞
