§19.26(i) General Formulas

ⓘ
Notes:
Addition theorems (and therefore duplication theorems) for the symmetric integrals are proved by Zill and Carlson (1970, §8). For other proofs of (19.26.1) see Carlson (1977b, §9.7) and Carlson (1978, Theorem 3). To prove (19.26.13) use (19.2.19) to show that 2⁢θ⁢RC⁡(σ2,σ2−θ)=ln⁡((σ+θ)/(σ−θ)), then apply this to all three terms. To prove (19.26.14) put z=y in (19.21.12) and use (19.20.8).
