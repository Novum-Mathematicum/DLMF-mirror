f⁡(ϵ,ℓ;r) is real and an analytic function of r in the interval −∞<r<∞, and it is also an analytic function of ϵ when −∞<ϵ<∞. This includes ϵ=0, hence f⁡(ϵ,ℓ;r) can be expanded in a convergent power series in ϵ in a neighborhood of ϵ=0 (§33.20(ii)).
