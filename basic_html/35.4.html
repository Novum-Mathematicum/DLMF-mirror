§35.4(i) Definitions

ⓘ

A partition κ=(k1,…,km) is a vector of nonnegative integers, listed in nonincreasing order. Also, |κ| denotes k1+⋯+km, the weight of κ; ℓ⁡(κ) denotes the number of nonzero kj; a+κ denotes the vector (a+k1,…,a+km).

The partitional shifted factorial is given by

35.4.1 [a]κ=Γm⁡(a+κ)Γm⁡(a)=∏j=1m(a−12⁢(j−1))kj,
