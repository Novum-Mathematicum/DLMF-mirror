As a function of z, with fixed k, each of the 12 Jacobian elliptic functions is doubly periodic, having two periods whose ratio is not real. Each is meromorphic in z for fixed k, with simple poles and simple zeros, and each is meromorphic in k for fixed z. For k∈[0,1], all functions are real for z∈ℝ.
