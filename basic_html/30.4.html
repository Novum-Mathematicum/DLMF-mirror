When γ2>0 𝖯𝗌nm⁡(x,γ2) is the prolate angular spheroidal wave function, and when γ2<0 𝖯𝗌nm⁡(x,γ2) is the oblate angular spheroidal wave function. If γ=0, 𝖯𝗌nm⁡(x,0) reduces to the Ferrers function 𝖯nm⁡(x):
