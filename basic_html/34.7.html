§34.7(ii) Symmetry

ⓘ
Keywords:
9⁢j symbols, symmetry
Permalink:
http://dlmf.nist.gov/34.7.ii
See also:
Annotations for §34.7 and Ch.34

The 9⁢j symbol has symmetry properties with respect to permutation of columns, permutation of rows, and transposition of rows and columns; these relate 72 independent 9⁢j symbols. Even (cyclic) permutations of either columns or rows, as well as transpositions, leave the 9⁢j symbol unchanged. Odd permutations of columns or rows introduce a phase factor (−1)R, where R is the sum of all arguments of the 9⁢j symbol.

For further symmetry properties of the 9⁢j symbol see Edmonds (1974, pp. 102–103) and Varshalovich et al. (1988, §10.4.1).
