§10.56 Generating Functions

ⓘ
Keywords:
generating functions, spherical Bessel functions
A&S Ref:
10.1.39, 10.1.40 (modified) 10.2.30, 10.2.31
Notes:
To verify (10.56.1) and (10.56.2) show that each side of both equations satisfies the differential equation (2⁢t−z)⁢(d2w/dt2)+(dw/dt)=z⁢w via the first of (10.51.1) and (10.49.3), (10.49.5). Then check the initial conditions at t=0. (10.56.3) and (10.56.4) follow from (10.56.1) and (10.56.2) via (10.47.12); then (10.56.5) follows from (10.47.11).
